FAQ  

  • What if I have multiple addresses? Click to show answer

    Simply check the box marked "Add an additional mailing address." To open an account online, one of your addresses must be within our service area.

  • How will my email address be used? Click to show answer

    Your email address will be used to communicate with you regarding the status of your application and the associated promotion with this account, if applicable.

  • Why do you need my Social Security number? Click to show answer

    Your Social Security number will be used to verify your identity. BB&T is required by Federal law to obtain, verify and record information that identifies each person who opens an account.

  • Can I open an account if I am not a U.S. citizen? Click to show answer

    You must be a U.S. citizen or legal resident alien with a valid Social Security number to open an account with BB&T.

  • What is a co-applicant? Click to show answer

    A co-applicant is someone who shares ownership and equal financial responsibility of your account. All joint accounts will be opened online in your name as well as in your co-applicant's name. (You may only add one co-applicant to each account.)

  • Can I add more than one co-applicant to my account? Click to show answer

    No, you may only add one co-applicant to your application, regardless of the number of accounts you are applying for today.

  • Why can't I change my state in the address section? Click to show answer

    If you don't see your state listed as an option, then you are out of our service area at this time.
